## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help improve website functionality, security, and user experience. Cookies may be set by us (“first-party cookies”) or by third-party services (“third-party cookies”).

## 2. How We Use Cookies

We may use cookies to:

- Ensure the website functions properly
- Analyze how visitors use the website
- Improve performance and user experience
- Enhance security
- Remember user preferences (where applicable)

We do not use cookies to collect personally identifiable information unless explicitly provided by you.

## 3. Types of Cookies We Use

### 3.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the basic operation of our website and cannot be disabled. They include:

- Security and authentication cookies
- Session management cookies
- Load-balancing and performance cookies

### 3.2 Analytics and Performance Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with the site, such as:

- Pages visited
- Time spent on pages
- Browser and device information

Tools we may use include (but are not limited to):

- Google Analytics
- Vercel Analytics

### 3.3 Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization, such as:

- Remembering preferences
- Improving navigation

### 3.4 Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies may be set by external services integrated into our site, such as:

- Embedded videos
- Social media integrations
- External analytics or tracking tools

These third parties may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies.

## 4. Managing Cookies

You can accept, reject, or modify cookie preferences through:

- Your browser settings
- Cookie banners or controls provided on our website (if applicable)

Most browsers allow you to:

- Delete cookies
- Block cookies
- Receive notifications before cookies are stored

Disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

- Essential cookies are used based on **legitimate interests**.

You may withdraw consent at any time through your browser or cookie settings.
